DEFORESTATION
All around the world, people are cutting down forests faster than they can grow.

Every day, roughly 10 million trees are cut down

This rate is unsustainable. Without action, the number will continue to rise, and our forests will continue to be destroyed permanently.

Deforestation is destroying the environment

The destruction of forests destroys the habitat for many species of wildlife, causing them to become endangered or extinct. In addition, the loss of large numbers of trees worsens climate change because there are fewer trees to absorb carbon dioxide.

Violating the rights of indigenous people

Deforestation destroys the homes of indigenous people, forcing them out of their own land and their way of life. Healthy forests provide homes for 1.6 billion people worldwide.
